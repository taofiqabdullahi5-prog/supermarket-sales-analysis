# Restaurant Sales & Customer Spending Analysis

A business-focused exploratory data analysis (EDA) project that investigates restaurant sales, customer spending patterns, and tipping behavior using Python and pandas.

---

## Problem Statement

Restaurant management wants to improve profitability by understanding customer spending habits, service quality, and operational performance.

This project answers business questions using transaction-level restaurant data.

---

## Objectives

- Calculate total revenue
- Measure average customer spending
- Analyze tipping behavior
- Explore customer demographics
- Generate business recommendations

---

## Key Results

✔ Total Revenue: $4,827.77

✔ Average Customer Spending: $19.79

✔ Average Tip Percentage: 16.08%

✔ Strong positive relationship between Total Bill and Tip

Regression Equation:

Tip = 0.11 × Total Bill + 0.92

## Exploratory Analysis

---

## Business Insights

- Higher bills generally produce higher tips.
- Average customers spend approximately $20.
- Average tipping rate is approximately 16%.
- Customer spending shows a positive linear relationship with tipping.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Encourage higher-value orders through meal bundles.
- Focus marketing on high-spending customer groups.
- Continue monitoring tipping trends as an indicator of customer satisfaction.
